Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) Project
Led the development of an innovative Electronic Voting Machine designed to enhance transparency and efficiency in the voting process. The project featured fingerprint-based voter authentication, real-time voting status updates via Blynk, and results display. This socially impactful project was awarded Most Socially Relevant Project at the VIT-AP University Expo in the academic year (2024-25).
